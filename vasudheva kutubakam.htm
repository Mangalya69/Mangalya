India has got the presidency of G20 for fy 2023-2024. Every host nation gets a chance to select a theme of their upcoming summits. India being the host nation One Earth, One Family, One Future. The idea of this theme from an ancient sanskrit saying "Vasudheva Kutumbakam". It signals the need for unity of purpose and unity of action.
